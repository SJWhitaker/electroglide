ABOUT.


A spacious, versatile, typically styled step-through moped, designed to tackle everyday use with ease and comfort. Suitable for all ages and both sexes, this bike is tough and robust and will withstand the daily commute or trips in to town with ease. Boasting a powerful 3000w motor and the 72v 30ah Lithium battery, this bike is as fast and dynamic as you’re going to get. A useful rear metal rack is great for those needing to attach a rear pannier / back box for transporting luggage and comes with deep tread all weather tyres for maximum grip and assurance. For anybody looking for a sensible commuter / city bike, this is certainly the one.
