Blog 2




Finally, the snow, rain and sub-zero temperatures have passed, meaning our roadshows can commence!

Our vision has always been to supply affordable commuter bikes to city centre workers, so what better way is there to showcase the Electroglide bikes than parking one in the middle of the office floor?

Starting in Sheffield, we have our demo bike in one of Sheffield’s largest call centres for two weeks, across two sites.

Why did we approach this particular business?

With several hundred employees and located in the heart of Sheffield city centre where traffic is heavy during typical commute times, we wanted to offer a solution to reduce their commute journey time-wise, eradicate their commute costs regarding fuel, and for those with an eye on the environment - an ethical mode of transport that removes city centre pollution.

We are constantly seeking new businesses to showcase our bikes, so if you work somewhere that may be interested in offering employees an alternative, we would love to hear from you.

Here’s to the sunshine!

Hello and welcome to our fabulous, new and exciting business, bringing the best in road legal electric moped / scooters from all around the world, to the UK market.

This company has been formed by 2 business partners after a successful 9 year working relationship in a separate nationwide technology company that helps UK businesses achieve great things.

Both with a very keen passion for motorbikes and the outdoors, the invention of Electroglide Ltd was a natural progression bringing our 2-wheeled passions and business experience together.

Electroglide Ltd is undoubtedly an exciting business, but with serious environmental undertones. It’s incredibly important we as consumers take charge of looking after the environment, where possible, to protect the world for our generations to come.

This website will see many improvements over the coming months, as will our range of bikes, meaning we will become a ‘one-stop shop’ for all styles of road legal bikes in the UK.

The future is now!

Best wishes, Team Electroglide.
