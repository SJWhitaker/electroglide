ABOUT US.


The conception of Electroglide Ltd was initiated by the need for low emission transport in the wake of the new government changes relating to air pollution caused by petrol and diesel combustion engines – the sale of both are to be stopped by 2040 in the UK, if not before.


We are already witnessing London leading the way with the introduction of the ‘toxicity charge’ taking effect from the 23rd October 2017, to reduce air pollution in the city. Following soon after the toxicity charge, the London Mayor is to widen this ‘zone’ to within the north and south circular roads, making it the world’s first ‘ultra-low emission zone’ intended to control air pollution, for the good of people’s health.


London are not the only UK city making changes – it’s reported that Leeds, Birmingham, Nottingham, Derby and Southampton are planning their own low emission zones to include daily charges, as they are the worst affected cities from vehicle pollution, behind London – this will spread country wide soon enough.


In spite of the air pollution issues in the UK, there’s a clear need for low or emission free vehicles, so Electroglide Ltd is the company that brings worldwide manufacturers and the general public together. We are not restricted to who we work with, meaning we source vehicles from only the best manufacturers, no matter where they are in the world, as long as we feel they are suitable.

OUR AIM.


To supply stylish, emission free, road legal vehicles that fall in to the ‘affordable’ price category, giving most people the option of a greener mode of transport.


Finally, Electroglide vehicles are fully electric, meaning there’s very few moving parts to service, no petrol or oil to fill up regularly, no noise so you won’t upset your neighbours, and ZERO ROAD TAX making an Electroglide a perfect choice for anybody looking to save money and help protect the environment.
