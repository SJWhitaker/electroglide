ABOUT.


The classic Italian design, made famous by a well-known Italian scooter brand. This bike is a real eye-catcher and puts a smile on everyone’s face with its wow factor. It’s built to a fantastic standard with a real solid, yet nimble feel when riding. This is the perfect commuter / city bike for those with style in mind and who have maybe once dreamed of being a part of the ‘mod’ life. Benefitting from the removable lithium battery, this model can even house 2 batteries, for those looking for a better range. A convenient storage space is available in the rear right wheel arch. Designed with all ages and both males and females in mind. The fashionista’s choice!
