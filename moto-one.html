ABOUT.


A cutting edge, new age designed bike, aimed at the younger market who aim to make a statement. Incredibly comfortable to ride with fantastic power, acceleration and handling, this bike is a real head-turner and talking point. Housing the powerful 3000w motor and a 72v 30ah silicon battery, this bike will transport the younger generation to college or work in both style and grace. A (very) useful storage compartment is hidden under the dummy petrol tank lid. Finally, this model has the marvellous chunky style tyres that promote superb grip and another edgy design talking point.
