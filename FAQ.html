What licence is required to ride an Electroglide?

Our Electroglide bikes are the equivalent to 50cc petrol mopeds, therefore they are restricted to 45kph. A CBT (compulsory basic training) is generally required, as are L plates, unless you fall in to a particular category as set out on the following government website - https://www.gov.uk/motorcycle-cbt

How many miles will I get per charge?

This all depends on the rider’s weight, but a 30-mile range would err on the side of caution. Further distances can be achieved, depending on the weather and road conditions, as well as the average speed and weight of the rider.

What pre-delivery checks are carried out?

We have an in-house technician who completes a detailed checklist, before the bikes are released to the public. They are imported to EEC standard, however we like to double check everything from top to bottom.

What after care and support will I receive once purchased?

We endeavour to support our customers to our best ability. We have a mechanic available at all times for troubleshooting and general enquiries. Should there be an issue with a bike, we will assess the situation and handle it accordingly, by sending a technician to you or by sourcing a local bike mechanic to help with the issue. We stock spare parts however some things may need to be imported however once the business grows in size this will become much faster and more efficient. We do not however anticipate many issues with our bikes due to their simple nature and few moving parts. Please be assured, we will always do our best to rectify ASAP.

What warranty is offered by Electroglide?

We offer a 12 – 24 month warranty on batteries, depending on which the bike has installed. The bikes themselves come with 12 months warranty. All claims will be carefully assessed and handled in a professional and timely manner.

How do I register the vehicle with DVLA?

You can either do this yourself via the relevant government website or we can do this for you. It’s a fixed £80 fee to register this type of vehicle.

What does the service schedule look like for maintenance?

We give you a recommended service schedule, however due to their low complexity, this is basic and inexpensive.

Is there a delivery option?

We are able to offer delivery for a small fee to cover any costs.

Do you offer a part exchange for petrol or electric bikes?

We will certainly explore the option of part exchange.
